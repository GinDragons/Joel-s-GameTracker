The Best Dragon Quest Games of All Time (According to Me)

My top 3 favorite Dragon Quest games of all-time.

Dragon Quest V

The best dragon quest I have played so far - it has monster recruiting, you go through the hero's entire life and you even have kids!

Dragon Quest XI

The "first" dragon quest chronologically, also the most widely available and gosh, it has the most amazing graphics.

Dragon Quest VIII

Jessica Albert. Nuff said.
